AI-Driven Scheduling Tool (EAPilot)

Artifact: Product Requirements Document (PRD) | Focus: Backend Logic & UX

Authored a detailed PRD for a scheduling tool designed to resolve cross-timezone conflicts. Proposed a flexible UI solution with backend logic requirements, aiming to increase setup completion and retention rates.

Prioritization & Scope (Defending the MVP)

Applied RICE Scoring and Story Mapping to define a strict MVP. To ensure Speed to Market, I ruthlessly cut features that did not directly solve the core user pain point of timezone miscalculations.

✅ In Scope (V1 Backbone)
  • Automated timezone detection.
  • Google Calendar 2-way sync.
  • Basic conflict resolution logic.
🚫 Out of Scope (V2 Ribs)
  • "Golden Window" AI Suggestions: Pushed to V2. High engineering effort; Cost of Delay threatened launch date.
  • Custom Branding: Pushed to V2. Identified as a "vitamin," not a core "painkiller."
