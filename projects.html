EAPilot: AI Calendar Sync

Focus: Technical requirements, AI integration, and Agile methodologies.

The Challenge: Executive Assistants lose 2–4 hours weekly on "reactive rescheduling." The lack of automated buffer zones and timezone detection leads to executive burnout and high manual overhead to fix the ripple effect of late-running meetings.

The Execution: Authored a detailed Product Requirement Document (PRD) for an AI-driven scheduling tool. Defined User Stories, Acceptance Criteria, and Technical Constraints to resolve cross-timezone conflicts.

Brassmoney: Target Savings Feature Improvement

Focus: User research, UX optimization, and metrics.

The Challenge: A 20% setup abandonment trend linked to inflexible deduction timing (fixed at 8:30 AM), creating friction for users whose cash flow did not align.

The Execution: Conducted targeted user research with 20+ fintech users. Proposed a flexible UI solution with backend logic requirements for variable scheduling, aiming to increase setup completion and retention rates.
