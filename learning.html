My PM Learning Journey

Documenting my transition from Executive Assistant to Product Manager through structured sprints, industry frameworks, and practical application.

Module 2: Prioritization & Scope

Defending the MVP

Core Concepts: RICE Scoring, Story Mapping, Cost of Delay, and Minimum Viable Product (MVP) definitions.

Key Takeaway: Mastered the ability to map a user journey to define a "Backbone" (V1) while actively pushing back against "Ribs" (V2) to prevent scope creep and prioritize Speed to Market.

Module 1: User Research & Discovery

The Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) Framework

Core Concepts: Problem Discovery, Non-Leading Questions, and Unarticulated Needs.

Key Takeaway: Learned to look beyond demographics to understand the functional, social, and emotional "jobs" a user is hiring a product to do, effectively separating "vitamins" from "painkillers."
