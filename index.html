About Me

As an Associate Product Manager, I excel at bringing structure to early-stage ideas. Right now, I'm managing the end-to-end documentation and workflow for a new digital product, bridging the gap between the founder's vision and the developer's code.

I came to Product Management from a 4-year career as an Executive Assistant. If you think about it, being an EA is a lot like being a PM—you manage chaos, act as the communication bridge between different teams, and make sure things actually get done on time. Today, I'm combining those hard-earned operational skills with my hands-on product experience. My goal is to join an agile tech team where I can build intuitive, user-focused software that users actually care about.
